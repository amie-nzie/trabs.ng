ABOUT OUR
COMPANY

Peace Mass Transit (PMT) Ltd is a road transport company in Nigeria that provides amazing travel services to clients. The transport company which is a subsidiary of Peace Group, Nigeria, was founded in 1995. Peace Mass Transit started operations with only 2 buses at UNN, Nsukka. However, the company currently boasts of a fleet of nearly 2,000 buses including Foton and Toyota Hummer buses

Travel with ease and comfort with us. Obtain tickets at your own leisure. Your comfort is guaranteed and assured in taking you to your destination. It is a kept promise!
