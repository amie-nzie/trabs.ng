ABOUT TRABS.NG

Trabs.ng was borne out of the need to have a unified online ticket booking and management platform in West Africa. It engages the dynamics of Technology to create an enabling platform for online bus ticket booking and management.

Company Background

Trabs.ng originates from Nigeria and has over 300 transport companies registered under the platform

Our Vision

To be the foremost online bus ticket booking and management platform in West Africa.

Our Mission

To engage the dynamics of technology to creating an enabling and convenient platform for online bus ticket booking and management
